Rice and Beans

Description

Red Beans and Rice is a timeless Southern comfort dish that combines tender red kidney beans, aromatic spices, and fluffy white rice into a hearty and flavorful meal. This recipe is a delightful fusion of Creole and Cajun flavors, bringing a taste of New Orleans right to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Water
  • 1/2 Lb Dry Pinto Beans, Sorted and Rinsed
  • 2 Small Onions, Quartered
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 1 tsp Ham Base
  • 1 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; add pinto beans, onions, garlic, ham base, and salt. Stir and return to a boil. Cover, turn off heat, and let stand for 1 hour.
  2. Return to a boil; reduce heat and let simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until beans are tender or have reached desired doneness, 40 to 50 minutes.
